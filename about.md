Hi all! 

Welcome to visit my website. 

I am currently a third-year Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Statistics at Purdue University, working under the supervision of Professor Qifan Song. Prior to this doctoral program, I earned a master's degree in Applied Statistics under the supervision of Professor Taeyoung Park at Yonsei University. After that, I gained practical experience as a data scientist at Datarize.

My current research focus revolves around the ethical aspects of machine learning, particularly in the domains of fairness and privacy. I am interested in developing methods to control and enhance the morality of machine learning algorithms by harnessing statistical perspectives. Additionally, I also have great interests in generative modeling and statistical inference applied to synthetic datasets, both of which would play crucial roles in trustworthy AI.
